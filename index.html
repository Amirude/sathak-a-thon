About Sathak-A-Thon

Sathak-A-Thon is an intercollegiate hackathon organized to encourage innovation, creativity, and collaboration among tech enthusiasts. Participants will work on real-world problems across various domains, showcasing their skills in design, development, and deployment.

Our Mission

At Sathak-A-Thon, our mission is to ignite innovation and empower students to transform ideas into real-world solutions. We believe in learning by doing, and we strive to foster a culture of creativity, collaboration, and tech-driven problem-solving.

500+ Participants

Join an electrifying community of over 500 tech enthusiasts ready to innovate, collaborate, and create magic during this dynamic event.

11 Existing Tracks

Choose from 11 exciting tracks like AI/ML, Web Development, IoT, Cybersecurity, and Blockchain to showcase your technical prowess.

Exciting Prizes

Win big! From cash rewards to tech goodies and internship opportunities, the prizes are as exciting as the event itself.

Hackathon Tracks

Track 1: Delivery Robot with Two-Wheeled Self-Balancing Bot (Problem ID: HACK5806)

A two-wheeled self-balancing robot that can autonomously deliver items within the college campus, such as documents, food, or books.

Track 2:Humanoid Robot (Problem ID: HACK-6820)

A humanoid robot capable of performing basic tasks such as guiding visitors, answering queries, or assisting in educational activities.

Track 3:Drone Security System (Problem ID: HACK-6055)

A drone-based security system that continuously patrols the college campus, providing live surveillance feeds and automatic alerting in case of suspicious activities.

Track 4:ERP System for College with Blackmarking Feature (Problem ID: HACK-6677)

A comprehensive ERP system tailored for colleges that manages student data, courses, schedules, and finances, with an added blackmarking feature for disciplinary actions.

Track 5:AI-Based Voice Bot for College (Problem ID: HACK-8694)

An AI-powered voice assistant that can answer common queries from students and staff about class schedules, exam results, events, and college policies.

Track 6: An AI-powered voice assistant that can answer common queries from students and staff about class schedules, exam results, events, and college policies.

This project involves creating a smart waste management system that uses IoT sensors to track the amount of waste in bins and send real-time data to a central system for waste collection scheduling.

Track 7:Smart Parking System with AI (Problem ID: HACK-2323)

A system that uses AI and sensors to detect available parking spaces in realtime and guide users to the nearest free spot. It can be integrated with a mobile app for easy tracking.

Track 8:College Health Monitoring System (Problem ID: HACK-2754)

A health monitoring system that tracks students' health metrics like body temperature, heart rate, and steps using wearable devices and updates a centralized database

Track 9:Virtual Reality (VR) Campus Tours (Problem ID: HACK-7127)

A VR application that allows prospective students to take a virtual tour of the college campus, exploring various facilities, departments, and common areas in an immersive environment.

Track 10: AI-Based Automated Class Scheduling System (Problem ID: HACK-2557)

An AI-driven system that generates optimal class schedules based on student preferences, professor availability, and room capacity to avoid conflicts and enhance classroom utilization

Track 11: Innovative Idea (Problem ID: HACK-2113)

Participants are encouraged to propose their own innovative solutions that address real-world problems within or beyond the campus. This could involve the use of automation, AI, IoT, sustainability, or any unique concept that adds value to society, education, or daily life.
